# Ingredients

* 50g sugar
* 25ml water
* 3 egg yolks
* 1 stick (1/2 cup) butter
* 1 tsp vanilla / orange water / blossom water

# Method
* Mix sugar and water over medium-high heat
* Whisk egg yolks in stand mixer
* Slowly add sugar-water mixture
* Mix
* Add butter
* Mix
* Add flavouring
