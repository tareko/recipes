# Ingredients

* 100g sugar
* 45ml water
* 5 egg yolks
* 1 cup Butter
* 1.5 tsp vanilla / orange water / blossom water

# Method
* Mix sugar and water over medium-high 
* Whisk egg yolks in stand mixer
* Slowly add sugar-water mixture
* Mix
* Add butter
* Mix
* Add flavouring
