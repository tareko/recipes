German Nougat or Gianduja
Ingredients

    500 gr part Hazelnuts
    500 gr icing sugar
    600 gr Milk Chocolate.
    And you need a professional blender.

Instructions

    Roast the nuts and remove the skin (with almonds remove skin before roasting).
    Put the nuts in the blender and blend them for some seconds. Add sugar and keep blending until smooth.It is quite warm now.
    Add the chocolate step by step and keep mixing.
    It should not get too hot, otherwise the chocolate begins to curdle.
